Plethora Magazine No. 11 – Anatomy of the Extreme ↑

The eleventh issue of Plethora Magazine pursues the boundaries of human capabilities and the extent to which our bodies and minds drive us to so seek out extreme achievements - often far beyond the rational threshold of natural physicality and sometimes in blatant disregard of our mortal confines.

K41 – “I see (it) because of you”

Based on M. C. Escher Visual approach to the idea that reality changes completely depending on how you look at it, this interpretation of "Relativity“ (1953) relates to the rewarding dynamic of relationships. The room elements propose stages of depth and complexity that shape your understanding of reality through the connection to another person. Further, the vibrant composition of color evokes a sensation of energy flow, as relationships bring in different perspectives, opening up the view to other dimensions.

Gotteswerk – Edition 5

This issue of "Gotteswerk" examines the relation between measure and time. Ornaments and illustrations enveloping the sleeve in medieval symbolic language refer to the deeper meaning between all of creation’s things and beings.
